Campus Life

School Gym

Was established in 1940

The school gym is very large, and is capable of holding the entire school in it. Because of this we hold all assemblies, concerts, and special visits in the gym.



School Library

Was established in 1940

The school library is where students can borrow books. Most of the time, library class will be held there. The library can be occupied by one class at a time.



School Playground

Was established in 1940

The school playground can hold 2 grades (6 classes) at a time. Maybe 5th grade and kindergarten are just the largest grades, but they go alone. There is a cycle. Kindergarten eats lunch. Then they go out for reccess. While they have reccess, 1st and 2nd grade have lunch. When kindergarten exits the playground, 1st and 2nd start reccess, and so on with 3rd, 4th, and 5th grade.

School Achievements

Class Debate on Class Constitution
Won debate and are making a class constitution.

In social studies, we were debating whether we should have a class constitution. We were debating on Thursday, and were finishing debating on Friday. I favored having a class constitution and my side won!

Won field day

On field day, blue team and white team compete to see who can win the most games. The whole school is involved, even teachers! Last year blue and white were pretty much tied and it came down to tug-of-war. My team, blue, won!

This year we tried to make the best science projects we could. A girl named Nayoon who is super smart came up with a better project than me. Mine was pretty good, I used household items to make a chemical reaction. I only earned 2nd place though.
